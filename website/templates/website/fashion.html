Summary

Fashion Designing is one of the most prevalent Design specialisations among scholars. Every year thousands of students apply at various fashion Design colleges across the country with an aspiration to become victorious fashion designers in future. If you too are aiming for a career in Fashion Design, you have anchored at the right place.

Fashion Design is one of the most creative course options available not only in India but also abroad. It is abundantly clear that the Fashion industry has grown by leaps and bounds in the past decade. Fashion Designing is a field of study that opens up a plethora of career prospects for those who choose the right course. It is essential to know some important aspects of Fashion Designing before enrolling in the course.

Despite the similarities between Fashion Design and Textile Design, there are differences as well. Fashion Design teaches students how to create clothes and lifestyle accessories. Textile Design teaches students how to create structures and designs for knitted, woven, printed & surface ornamented fabrics, etc.
