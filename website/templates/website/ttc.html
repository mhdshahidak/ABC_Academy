Summary

Montessori training is a transformational journey that deepens one's understanding of children's potential and their ability to transform the world. Dr Maria Montessori gave special consideration to the formative psychic powers of the young child, which lead to all-around development in early childhood development (ECE and ECD).

This is an immaculate course for those who are inquisitive in Montessori Teaching. The course material is devised to comprehend and enforce the technology of Montessori. As there is a considerable demand for proficient Montessori in India and abroad.

This course is suggested to all proficient teachers of young children who wish to acquire insight into the best way to individualize their teaching curriculum, so that children of mixed proficiencies may work at their own pace. Teachers consider this course as an up-gradation and implemented the Montessori methodology in conventional schools.

Main targets

The course is mainly focused on the concepts of Montessori principles, such as 'the absorbent mind, sensitive periods and the child tendencies, the subconscious power, which guide the child in self-construction and privilege to comprehend on its own within a structured environment. Dr Maria Montessori described childhood as the process of how the child develops his or her adult personality in accordance with a hidden yet determined plan of nature.

BENEFITS OF THE COURSE

  • Flexible curriculum.
  • Help to arrange a cohesive and scientific environment which aids in self-learning.
  • Authorizes to teach at own pace, scientific technique of teaching, reality orientated and specific materials – (sense of order).
  • Preferences on activities according to the inner needs of the children.
  • Development of child-centred learning environment, self-education through self-correction methods.
  • Recognition of sensitive periods in each child.
  • Creating multi-sensory materials to develop a specific skill should be a priority.
  • Dig up the benefits of yoga, mindfulness and nourishment into the children.
